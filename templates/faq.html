Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a movie or event ticket?

Browse from the homepage, choose your preferred movie or event, select your time and seats, and follow the checkout process.

2. Can I view my booking details after confirmation?

Yes, go to the “My Bookings” section from your dashboard or profile. Click “View” next to any booking to see full details and your QR code.

3. How do I cancel a booking?

Go to ‘My Bookings’, click “View”, then choose “Cancel Booking” — only available for upcoming bookings.

4. How will I receive my refund?

Once cancelled, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

5. Can I cancel on the day of the event?

Cancellations are allowed only if the event or movie hasn’t started yet.

6. What’s the difference between Upcoming and Past bookings?

Future bookings with confirmed status appear under “Upcoming”. Cancelled or expired ones move automatically to “Past Bookings”.

7. Why does a booking say “Cancelled” in red?

That means you’ve successfully cancelled that booking. A refund is on its way.

8. How can I reach support?

Click on “Contact Us” in the top navigation bar to send us a message directly.

9. Will I get a confirmation email?

Yes! You’ll receive an email with booking and refund info upon confirmation or cancellation.

10. What is the QR code used for?

The QR code is your digital ticket. Present it at the venue for check-in.
