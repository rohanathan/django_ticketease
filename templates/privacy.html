Privacy Policy

At TicketEase, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 When you register, purchase tickets, or interact with our platform, we collect the following information:
- Name, email address, and phone number
- Payment details (processed securely via third-party payment gateways)
- Event preferences and browsing behavior

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 We use your information for the following purposes:
 - To process ticket bookings and payments
 - To send booking confirmations and event-related notifications
 - To improve our services and provide personalized recommendations
 - To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

3.1 TicketEase does not sell your personal information.
3.2 We may share data with:
 - Event organizers for ticket validation and event access
 - Payment processors to facilitate transactions
 - Marketing partners for promotional offers (with user consent)

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

4.1 We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.
4.2 You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

5.1 We implement strong security measures to protect user data.
5.2 Despite security efforts, no online platform is completely secure, and users should take precautions when sharing sensitive information.

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Users have the right to:
- Access and update their personal data
- Request data deletion or restriction of processing
- Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Children’s Privacy

7.1 TicketEase does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
7.2 Parents or guardians should contact us if they believe their child’s information has been shared.

8. Policy Updates

8.1 TicketEase reserves the right to update this policy. Changes will be communicated via email or website notifications.

9.Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@ticketease.com.
